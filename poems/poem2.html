'There's something here with me,' he whispered, afraid - A shape in the darkness, the shadow it made. It moved in the silence - it turned in the night - It crawled and it crept in a flicker of light. It came from seclusion - it slipped throgh the trees - A voice in the gloaming, a sound on the breeze - A crowding of creatures, he saw with dismay. He hid on the ground till they all went away.
